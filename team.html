Our Team

Bringing together clinical expertise, molecular biology, and translational science to advance hearing restoration.

Our interdisciplinary team combines clinical expertise, molecular biology, and translational research to advance hearing restoration. Embedded within the Department of Otolaryngology at University Hospital Tübingen, we benefit from direct access to patient populations and clinical infrastructure, enabling us to keep therapeutic relevance at the centre of everything we do.

HL

Prof. Hubert Löwenheim

MD PhD — FEBORL
Principal Investigator

Hubert Löwenheim is Professor of Otolaryngology and Chair of the Department of Otolaryngology — Head & Neck Surgery at University Hospital Tübingen. A physician-scientist trained in molecular biology and clinical otology, he has devoted more than two decades to elucidating the mechanisms of sensory hair cell loss and regeneration in the mammalian inner ear. His research spans fundamental investigations in model organisms, translational gene therapy programmes, drug screening platforms, and direct clinical innovation in cochlear implantation and vestibular rehabilitation. Professor Löwenheim serves on multiple international scientific advisory boards and is a principal investigator in several EU- and DFG-funded consortia. As a practicing ENT surgeon, he ensures that every research question in the laboratory remains anchored to the clinical realities faced by patients with hearing loss.
